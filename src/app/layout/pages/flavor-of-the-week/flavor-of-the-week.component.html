About District Pour House + Kitchen

District. Pour House + Kitchen in Historic Waldo Offers unique craft brews and libations, original American food and local flair.

Our Team is made by talented and motivated people. They all share one thing: The Love for Food!,

We feature 34 infused spirits, including three rotating/seasonal selections. We have over 100 bottled beers and 13 on draught. Our Libations menu is crafted around the infusions. We use only the finest and freshest ingredients in all of our infusions. All of our recipes are rigorously taste tested to tantalize your palate. Our food menu is New/Progressive American cuisine, highlighting flavors from multiple regions to satisfy everyone's cravings. We like to consider our place upscale, yet casual. Romantic, but edgy.
